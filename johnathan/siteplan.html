Purpose

The purpose of this site is meant to be a humorous site with absurd humor. It will accomplish this through a variety of information of assorted Johnathans.

Audience

The audience of the site is children to young adults with an absurd sense of humor.

Content

Home page

This website is all about people named Johnathan. This website will provide facts, jokes, and ideas all about various people with his name. The website will treat all this information as if it belonged to a single person as a joke. We hope to amuse users with the ridiculousness of the premise and the blatant ignorance of how information is relayed to the viewer. This home page will provide an introduction to the concept of the website and make it more clear what is going on.

Images for the Home page

Facts

This page will provide interesting facts about various Johnathans, it will, as with the rest of the website, deliver them as if it is a single man named Johnathan. It will go over impressive accomplishments by various men named Johnathan, and make him out to be a spectacular person with many talents. Users will find him having done many things, some even contradictory to each other.

Images for the Page 2

Timeline

This page will provide a timeline of Johnathan’s life, with no clear order of events and stretching centuries. It will paint a confusing picture of his life and experiences in order to continue the image that he is a single person despite everything proving otherwise. This page will find users treading through an undated timeline with events that don’t paint any clear idea of when the events actually happened.
