It's him! The man himself.

This website is all about people named Johnathan. This website will provide facts, jokes, and ideas all about various people with his name. The website will treat all this information as if it belonged to a single person as a joke. We hope to amuse users with the ridiculousness of the premise and the blatant ignorance of how information is relayed to the viewer. This home page will provide an introduction to the concept of the website and make it more clear what is going on.
